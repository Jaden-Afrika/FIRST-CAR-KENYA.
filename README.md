# FIRST-CAR-KENYA.
# ABOUT:
First Car Kenya is here to take the stress out of buying your first car. It’s an online marketplace designed for anyone in Kenya who wants a simple, easy way to find a reliable starter vehicle. Just search for any make or model, and you’ll get instant info—car details, year, fuel type, and more—right on the spot. The goal? Keep things easy, clear, and quick so you can pick your car without the usual headaches.

# WEBSITE DESIGN:
When you land on the homepage, there’s a search tool allowing you to type in any vehicle, and First Car Kenya instantly shows you the model, year, fuel type, and a crisp professional image, all thanks to CarsQuery and Pexels. You can slide through different sections like Inventory or Financing, getting a good look at everything you need to make a decision.

The site’s got a no-nonsense design, thanks to a Software Engineer who knows how to keep things clean and user-friendly. Everything’s organized, easy to browse, and looks good. There’s nothing fussy or complicated, just smooth performance built on reliable HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
